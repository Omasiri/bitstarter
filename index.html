Improved Diagnostics & Patient Outcomes

Novadge CarePlus+ makes complete health records readily available at the point of care from anywhere enabling providers to diagnose patients problems sooner, make informed decisions and deliver better health care services.

Increase efficiency

With Novadge CarePlus+, electronically recording, modifying and retrieving a patients problem list for longitudinal care is only a few clicks away. This helps save health care providers significant amount of time and stress as Novadge CarePlus+ presents the required information instantly.

Lab integration

Novadge CarePlus+ includes a Lab information management module for making lab test requests and storing lab test results for patients. This affords health care providers instant and easy access to medical lab results. Novadge CarePlus+ keeps all of a patient's health information in one place thereby reducing duplication of tests, saving time and resources.

Pharmacy integration

Novadge CarePlus+ includes a Pharmacy information management module for managing electronic prescriptions and drug inventory. Novadge CarePlus+ e-prescribing system can help eliminate illegible prescriptions and oral miscommunication and improve outcomes (decision support).

Better patient engagement

Novadge CarePlus+ provides education and reference resources to health care providers and patients through integration with credible information sources. By making educational and reference resources available, decision making becomes easier and leads to better outcomes.

Better collaboration

Novadge CarePlus+ conforms to international standards for recording patient data, problems, prescriptions and lab requests. This will help health care providers collaborate easily while rendering health care services.

Document Management

Novadge CarePlus+ can reduce the amount of time you spend doing paperwork. Novadge CarePlus+ provides a portable document management system for storing scanned patient documents. Eg. Xray scans, tests, etc. This enables quick and on-demand access to patients documents.

Smarter billing

Novadge CarePlus+ provides an accounting/ billing module for recording service invoices and payments by patients. This will help streamline service delivery, billing and payment.

Decision Support

Novadge CarePlus+ can help crosscheck patient prescriptions and medications at the point of care for drug to drug and drug-allergy interaction, and notify the provider when it detects problems with prescriptions or medication for a patient. Novadge CarePlus+ Decision support contributes to better health care delivery.

Research and Insight

Whether you are a health care policy maker or a scientist trying to improve the world around you; the data you gather with Novadge CarePlus+ combined with analytical tools could provide highly valuable insight. Novadge CarePlus+ gets better and better.

Improve public health.

Gather health information about the entire population of patients you serve and Novadge CarePlus+ would store, secure and help you analyze it. With Novadge CarePlus+, you can monitor various trends, gain insight, look more meaningfully at your patients needs and make informed decisions.

Reduce Paper, Help Our Planet.

Reduce the amount of time you spend doing paperwork. Tasks such as filling out forms and processing billing requests represent a significant percentage of health care costs. Novadge CarePlus+ helps streamlines these tasks and can significantly decrease cost.
